--- title: "Sinusitis diagnosis X-ray image generation using GAN" excerpt: "

Our project focused on addressing the limitations of real medical image data by creating a synthetic dataset. We collected and purified PNS data from medical institutions, using it to train state-of-the-art GANs, including StylGAN2-ADA, ProGAN, and Hybrid Diffusion-GAN models, to create realistic medical images. The goal was to enhance accessibility for researchers and practitioners, providing them with a valuable resource for their work. This project exemplifies our dedication to overcoming data constraints and promoting innovation in healthcare and AI." collection: portfolio ---
